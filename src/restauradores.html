Restauradores is a boutique collection of luxury apartments located in the heart of central Lisbon.

The building has been transformed into a number of one to tree-bedroom luxury apartments.

The 10 apartments size range from 65 sqm to 122 sqm and have been designed to blend comfort, privacy and city-centre living.
